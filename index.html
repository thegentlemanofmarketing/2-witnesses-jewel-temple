Stage Scenario Customization Tips
Attract AI-generated follow-up for web chat: A prospective seeker submits a question at 8pm. Even if unavailable, they get a personalized response instantly.
  • Update AI prompt for use cases like session bookings or esoteric inquiries.
  • Example: "Thanks for reaching out about [topic]! I'd love to help — would you like to schedule a call?"
Convert & Engage Engage web chat leads: A lead reaches out and receives confirmation by SMS/email. CRM logs contact, and task is created to follow up.
  • Tag by inquiry type ("Consultation Request", "Course Inquiry").
  • Customize task title: "Follow up to book appointment with [Name]".
  • Adjust reminder timing (e.g., within 4 hours).
Retain Add to marketing campaign: A lead that didn't convert immediately is added to an automated email series with teachings or event updates.
  • Choose campaigns like "Weekly Temple Updates" or "Frequency Tips".
  • Add tags like "Warm Lead" or "First-Time Seeker".
Nurture Lead follow-up: A lead receives pricing or info but hasn't responded. Three days later, they get a warm follow-up.
  • Trigger when moved to "Info Sent".
  • Example: "Hi [Name], just checking in — happy to answer any questions. Ready when you are!"
Reputation Automatically request reviews: After completing a consultation, the client receives a review request with a thank-you.
  • Trigger on tasks like "Session Complete".
  • Example: "It was a pleasure guiding you. We'd really appreciate your feedback: [link]"
